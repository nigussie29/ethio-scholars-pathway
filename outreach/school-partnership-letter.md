# School Partnership Letter

Subject: Student Nomination Opportunity – Ethio Scholars Pathway

Dear School Director/Teacher,

My name is Nigussie Guluma, and I am developing Ethio Scholars Pathway, a program designed to help talented Ethiopian high school students prepare for U.S. college admission and scholarship opportunities.

We are preparing to launch a small pilot program for motivated students who are academically strong, financially in need, and interested in studying in the United States.

The program will provide online guidance in academic English, college application preparation, scholarship readiness, essay writing, and mentorship.

We respectfully invite your school to nominate students who may benefit from this opportunity.

Please note that this program does not guarantee admission, scholarships, visas, or travel. Our goal is to prepare students to compete strongly and honestly for available opportunities.

Thank you for supporting talented Ethiopian students.

Sincerely,

Nigussie Guluma  
Founder, Ethio Scholars Pathway
