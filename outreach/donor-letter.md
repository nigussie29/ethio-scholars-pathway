# Donor Letter

Subject: Help Support Talented Ethiopian Students

Dear [Name],

I am developing Ethio Scholars Pathway, a program designed to help talented Ethiopian high school students prepare for U.S. college admission and scholarship opportunities.

Many students have strong academic potential but lack access to test preparation, internet support, application guidance, mentorship, and document preparation.

Your support can help students participate in a structured six-month preparation program focused on English writing, college research, scholarship readiness, and mentorship.

Even a small gift can support internet access, study materials, application preparation, or student advising.

Thank you for considering support for this mission.

Sincerely,

Nigussie Guluma  
Founder, Ethio Scholars Pathway
