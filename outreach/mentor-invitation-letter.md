# Mentor Invitation Letter

Subject: Invitation to Mentor Ethiopian High School Students

Dear [Name],

I am launching Ethio Scholars Pathway, a program that helps talented Ethiopian high school students prepare for U.S. college admission and scholarship opportunities.

We are starting with a small pilot group of students and are looking for mentors who can support them with writing, college preparation, career guidance, interview practice, and encouragement.

Mentors may support students once or twice per month online. The goal is to help students understand the U.S. college system, build confidence, and prepare strong applications.

The program does not promise admission or scholarships. It focuses on preparation, mentorship, and honest guidance.

Please let me know if you would be interested in supporting this mission.

Sincerely,

Nigussie Guluma  
Founder, Ethio Scholars Pathway
