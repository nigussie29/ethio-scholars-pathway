# Student Selection Rubric

The selection process should be fair, transparent, and consistent. Students should be evaluated based on academic strength, motivation, financial need, leadership, and readiness for the program.

| Category | Points | Description |
|---|---:|---|
| Academic Performance | 25 | Grades, transcript, national exam results |
| Entrance Assessment | 25 | Math, English, reasoning, writing |
| Essay and Motivation | 20 | Clear goals, maturity, purpose |
| Financial Need | 15 | Need for support and opportunity gap |
| Leadership/Service | 10 | Community involvement, responsibility |
| Teacher Recommendation | 5 | Teacher support and character evidence |
| Total | 100 | Final score |

## Suggested Selection Levels

- 85–100: Strong acceptance candidate
- 75–84: Waitlist or interview candidate
- 65–74: Needs review
- Below 65: Not ready for pilot group

## First Pilot Target

Select 20 Ethiopian high school students for the first six-month pilot program.

## Important Fairness Rule

Do not select students only by grades. The program should also consider motivation, financial need, leadership, writing, and teacher recommendation.
