# Six-Month Scholarship Preparation Program

## Target

20 Ethiopian high school students

## Meeting Schedule

One online class per week, 90 minutes

## Communication

- WhatsApp or Telegram group for announcements
- Email for official documents
- Google Drive for resources
- Zoom or Google Meet for live sessions

---

# Month 1: Orientation and Goal Setting

- U.S. college system overview
- Scholarship types
- Student expectations
- Parent orientation
- Personal academic goal setting

# Month 2: Academic English and Writing

- Reading comprehension
- Academic vocabulary
- Essay writing
- Email communication
- Personal statement introduction

# Month 3: Math and Test Preparation

- Algebra review
- Functions
- Data analysis
- Word problems
- SAT-style math practice

# Month 4: College Research

- How to choose colleges
- Major and career planning
- Understanding tuition and financial aid
- College list building

# Month 5: Application Materials

- Personal statement
- Student resume
- Recommendation request
- Transcript preparation
- Activity list

# Month 6: Scholarship and Interview Readiness

- Scholarship application practice
- Mock interviews
- Final college list
- Family meeting
- Next-step plan

---

# Pilot Outcomes

By the end of six months, each student should have:

- Student profile
- College goal plan
- Resume/activity list
- Personal statement draft
- Scholarship search list
- Improved English writing sample
- Teacher recommendation plan
- Family next-step plan
