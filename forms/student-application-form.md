# Student Application Form

## Apply to Ethio Scholars Pathway

Ethio Scholars Pathway is accepting applications from Ethiopian high school students who are motivated, academically strong, and interested in preparing for U.S. college admission and scholarship opportunities.

## Before You Apply

Please prepare:

- Student full name
- School name
- Grade level
- City/region
- Parent or guardian contact
- Recent transcript or grade report
- National exam results, if available
- Teacher recommendation
- Short essay explaining your goals

## Student Commitment

Accepted students must agree to:

- Attend weekly online sessions
- Complete assignments on time
- Communicate respectfully
- Practice English writing and speaking
- Prepare seriously for college and scholarship applications
- Keep parents or guardians informed

---

# Form Questions

## Section 1: Student Information

1. Full name
2. Date of birth
3. Grade level
4. School name
5. City/region
6. Student phone number
7. Student email
8. Parent/guardian name
9. Parent/guardian phone number
10. Parent/guardian email, if available

## Section 2: Academic Information

1. Current grade average or GPA
2. Strongest subjects
3. Most difficult subjects
4. National exam result, if available
5. Upload transcript or grade report
6. Teacher name for recommendation
7. Teacher contact information

## Section 3: English and Technology Access

1. How would you rate your English level?
   - Beginner
   - Intermediate
   - Advanced
2. Can you attend online classes weekly?
3. Do you have access to a phone?
4. Do you have access to a computer?
5. Do you have regular internet access?
6. What support do you need most?

## Section 4: Student Motivation

1. Why do you want to study in the United States?
2. What subject or major do you want to study?
3. What problem in Ethiopia do you want to help solve in the future?
4. Describe one challenge you have overcome.
5. Why should Ethio Scholars Pathway select you?

## Section 5: Financial Need

1. Do you need financial support for application preparation?
2. What type of support would help you most?
3. Briefly explain your family financial situation.

## Section 6: Agreement

Students must agree:

- I will attend online sessions consistently.
- I will complete assignments honestly.
- I understand this program does not guarantee admission, scholarship, visa, or travel.
- I give permission for the program to review my academic documents.
- My parent/guardian knows I am applying.
