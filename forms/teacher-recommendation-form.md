# Teacher Recommendation Form

This form is used to help Ethio Scholars Pathway understand a student's academic ability, character, motivation, and readiness for a U.S. college preparation program.

## Questions

1. Student name
2. Teacher name
3. School name
4. Subject taught
5. How long have you known the student?
6. Rate the student's academic ability.
7. Rate the student's work ethic.
8. Rate the student's English communication.
9. Rate the student's leadership.
10. Rate the student's honesty and discipline.
11. What makes this student special?
12. What challenges has the student overcome?
13. Would you recommend this student for a U.S. college preparation program?
14. Teacher signature/name confirmation
15. Teacher contact information

## Rating Scale Suggestion

Use a 1 to 5 scale:

1. Needs significant support
2. Developing
3. Good
4. Very strong
5. Excellent
