# Mission, Vision, and Public Promise

## Working Name

**Ethio Scholars Pathway**

## Mission Statement

Ethio Scholars Pathway identifies talented Ethiopian high school students, prepares them for U.S. college admission, and connects them with scholarship opportunities through academic training, mentorship, and financial support.

## Vision

To build a trusted bridge between talented Ethiopian students and global educational opportunities, especially U.S. colleges and universities.

## Safe Public Promise

We do not promise admission, scholarships, visas, or travel to the United States. We prepare motivated Ethiopian students to compete strongly for U.S. college admission and scholarship opportunities.

## Core Values

1. Opportunity for talented students with financial need
2. Honest and transparent scholarship guidance
3. Academic excellence
4. Leadership and community service
5. Respect for students, parents, schools, mentors, and donors
6. Long-term service to Ethiopia

## Suggested Slogan

**From Talent to Global Opportunity**

## Logo Direction

Recommended logo concept:

- Open book at the bottom
- Bridge or pathway rising from the book
- Star above the path
- Colors: green, gold, and navy blue

Meaning:

- Book = education
- Pathway or bridge = journey from Ethiopia to global opportunity
- Star = scholarship, hope, and excellence
